How to PAY at Parking Garages

Step 1: Entry Ticket Dispenser

Upon entry to garage, push dispenser button on machine and Pull an entry ticket.

Step 2: Pay Station

Prior to exiting the garage, PAY fee at 1 of 2 walk-up pay stations by credit card, debit card or cash/coin.

Step 3: Exit Ticket Verifier

As you exit the garage, insert PAID ticket into the Exit Verifier machine.

*With Event flat rates, you may PAY before the event and hold your ticket for the Exit Ticket Verifier.
